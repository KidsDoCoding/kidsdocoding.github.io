KidsDoCoding!

Kids Do Coding! is aimed towards teaching 10 to 13 year old, beginning students the fundamental programming concepts that are necessary in today's computer-heavy world. We explore the various implementations of these concepts through the use of Python and we hope to build the foundation for a life long passion in computer science.
