Our Cause

Kids Do Coding! is aimed towards teaching beginner and intermediate students the fundamental programming concepts that are necessary in today's computer-heavy world. We explore the various implementations of these concepts through the use of Python and we hope to build the foundation for a life long passion in computer science.

FAQ

Should I attend the meetings alongside my child?

Parents are welcome to join in on our meetings! However, we will provide the necessary support to ensure that no student is left behind.


What age group is this targeted at?

KidsDoCoding! is targeted at studfents of ages 10-13 with little to no prior programming experience.
